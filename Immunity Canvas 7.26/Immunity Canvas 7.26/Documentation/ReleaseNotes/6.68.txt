*Release Notes*:

This release introduces two new exploits for Windows. Use ms11_032
to escalate privileges on XP, 2003, Vista, 2008 and Windows 7
and ms11_003 to exploit IE7 on the clientside.

Moreover, we include safari_navaction (for OSX x64 and Windows)
and CVE_2011_0182 that can be used to escalate privileges
to root on OSX 10.6.6 and below.
